Meatball Recipe

Description

Are you in a time crunch and don't know what cook for dinner for your family? Meatballs are your answer! Don't just take it from us, take it from our community who say they "loved this recipe!"

If you are looking to store the meatballs, you can place them in a shallow, airtight container and in the refrigerator for up to four days. Additionally, you can also freze them! Flash freeze the cooked meatballs on a baking sheet for a few hours or up to overnightm then transfer them to a freezer-safe container. You can also freeze the meatballs prior to cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1 cup freshly grated Romano cheese
  • 2 eeggs
  • 2 gloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons chopped Italian flat leaf parsley
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups stale Italian bread, crumbled
  • 1 1/2 cups lukewarm water
  • 1 cup olive oil

    • Steps

    1. Step 1

      Combine beef, veal, and pork in a large bowl. Mix in cheese, eggs, garlic, parsley, salt and pepper.

    2. Step 2

      Add bread crumbs and slowly mix in water, 1/2 cup at a time, until mixture is moist but still holds its shape (I usually use about 1 1/4 cups of water); shape into meatballs.

    3. Step 3

      Heat olive oil in a large skillet; add meatballs in batches and cook until browned on all side, slightly crisp, and cooked through, about 10 to 15 minutes. Drain on paper towls.
