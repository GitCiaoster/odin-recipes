Lasagna

Description

Tired of trying to cook lasagna but never having it cook all the way through? Have your kids said your lasagna can pass for a meatloaf? Look no further than here at world's best lasagna!

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below!

Ingredients

The Allrecipes community adores this lasagna recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredit list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • Meat: This super meat lasagna has sweet Italian Sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tables spoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leae,s salt, Ialian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

    • How to make Lasagna Step-By-Step

    Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna:

    1. Make the meat sauce.
    2. Cook the noodles.
    3. Make the ricotta mixture.
    4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
    5. Cover with foil and bake
    6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

    How to layer lasagna

    The detailed layer instructions can be found in the recipe below, but this is the order you'll follow:

    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozazarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaing Parmesan.
